What to do in Bieszczady?

The Bieszczady towns and villages may sound like sleepy little joints, but there is absolutely no dearth of activities for the tourist to indulge in. The area offers numerous opportunities for the outdoors kind of person. The most popular are water sports at Solina, hiking, mountain biking and yoga.

Hiking

Unique and unknown destination in the Polish Carpathians. Exceptional national park known for its fauna and many protected species of large European mammals. Exceptional evenings with tasty meals and with highland traditions.

Yoga

Bieszczady is a perfect place for finding the life balance. Close to the nature practicing more and more popular yoga and medidation.

Biking

An exciting cycling tours which explores beautiful and remote countryside. In the year 2002, 700 km of bicycle paths of varying degrees were marked.
