What to do in Bieszczady Mountains?

The Bieszczady towns and villages may sound like sleepy little joints, but there is absolutely no dearth of activities for the tourist to indulge in.
The area offers numerous opportunities for the outdoors kind of person. The most popular are hiking, mountain biking, yoga and water sports at Solina.

Hiking

The Bieszczady mountains differ from the other mountain ranges in Poland. They are famous from a distinctive grassland zone known as połonina/polonina/polonyna. This is believed to be a natural phenomenon, unique and unknown destination.

Yoga

Bieszczady is a perfect place for finding the balance in life. Far away from the city's rush and close to the nature. It is the perfect place for practicing, more and more popular, yoga, but also for medidation.

Biking

Bieszczady offers an exciting cycling tours which explores beautiful and remote countryside. Already, in the year 2002, 700 km of bicycle paths of varying degrees were marked. The bicycle routes are best know as "Green Bicycle Bieszczady”.

Water

Lake Solina is one of the most attractive places for sailors – with its beautiful landscapes and quiet fjords and coves. The area is also attractive to anglers. The must see on the list, is of course impressive dam.
