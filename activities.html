What to do in Bieszczady?

The Bieszczady towns and villages may sound like sleepy little joints, but there is absolutely no dearth of activities for the tourist to indulge in. The area offers numerous opportunities for the outdoors kind of person. The most popular are water sports at Solina, hiking and biking.
